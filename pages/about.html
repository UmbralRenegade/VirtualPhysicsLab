About Dynamax

Interactive Physics Simulations

What is Dynamax?

Dynamax is an interactive physics simulation platform that helps you visualize and understand fundamental concepts in classical mechanics. Manipulate variables in real-time and see how physical systems respond. Learn physics through hands-on experimentation.

Experiments Included

🎯 Projectile Motion

Launch objects with adjustable velocity and angle. Watch how gravity pulls them down while maintaining horizontal velocity. Learn about parabolic trajectories.

💥 Momentum Conservation

Observe collisions between objects and verify that momentum is always conserved. Test elastic and inelastic collisions with different masses.

〰️ Spring-Mass System

Explore simple harmonic motion with springs. Adjust spring stiffness, mass, and initial displacement to see periodic oscillations and energy exchange.

⚛️ Double Pendulum

Witness chaotic motion with an interconnected two-pendulum system. Small changes in initial conditions lead to drastically different outcomes.

Quick Start

  • Select an experiment from the home page
  • Adjust sliders to change parameters
  • Click "Start" or "Launch" to begin
  • Observe the simulation and calculated values
